About Solis Green Energy

At Solis Green Energy, we understand the importance of clean and renewable energy and are dedicated to providing the highest quality Solar PV Systems on the market. Our team of experienced engineers and technicians design and engineer the systems to meet the specific needs of each customer, and our skilled installers ensure that the systems are installed to the highest standards.

To provide accessible, clean and affordable solar energy solutions to homes and businesses across Kerala.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about solar energy

The cost depends on system size, brand, and complexity. A 3KW system starts from ₹1,50,000 after subsidies. We provide detailed quotations after site assessment.

Government offers 40% subsidy for up to 3KW and 20% for 3-10KW systems for residential installations. We handle all subsidy documentation.

Solar systems require minimal maintenance - primarily panel cleaning every 6 months and annual system checkups. We offer comprehensive AMC packages.
