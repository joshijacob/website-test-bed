About Solis Green India

Powering Kerala's sustainable future since September 2019

Our Story

Founded in September 2019, Solis Green India began with a simple mission: to make sustainable solar energy accessible and affordable for every home and business in Kerala.

Starting from our base in Thiruvalla, we've grown to become one of Kerala's most trusted solar energy providers, serving thousands of satisfied customers across multiple districts.

Our team of certified solar professionals combines technical expertise with local knowledge to deliver customized solutions that maximize savings and efficiency.
